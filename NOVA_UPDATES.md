# NOVA Color Palette - Bright & Energetic

## Primary Colors
- **Electric Violet**: #7c3aed
- **Hot Pink**: #ec4899  
- **Cyber Cyan**: #06b6d4
- **Bright Purple**: #a855f7

## Gradients
- Primary: `from-[#7c3aed] via-[#a855f7] to-[#ec4899]`
- Secondary: `from-[#7c3aed] via-[#ec4899] to-[#06b6d4]`
- Background: `from-[#f8f9fe] via-[#faf5ff] to-[#f0f9ff]`

## Usage
- Buttons: Vibrant gradients with bold shadows
- Cards: Clean white with colored borders and accents
- Progress bars: Animated gradients showing growth
- Icons: Gradient fills for visual pop
